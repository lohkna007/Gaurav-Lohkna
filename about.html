Hi, I'm Gaurav

Ivy League labs taught me to ask questions, AI taught me to find answers—and I’m just getting started.

Curious Beginnings

🧪 In 2018, I found myself inside the stone walls of Yale, not knowing that this curious detour into neuroscience research would ignite a much deeper question—how does intelligence really work? And can machines learn too?

The Foundations

🖥️ Back in Delhi, I pursued a degree in Computer Science. Not just for the code, but for the patterns. I explored not just what machines compute—but why humans create them in the first place.

The Acceleration

🚀 Getting into the Berkeley x Plaksha AI/ML program was like standing at the launchpad. I dove into applied ML, built a news recommender, and led teams that explored how to align AI with human intuition.

Healthcare and Responsibility

🏥 At Minfy, I built LLM-driven tools that helped hospitals cut discharge reporting time from 30 minutes to 2. That’s when AI became less about hype—and more about helping real people.

The Copilot Era

🤖 Today at HRS Group, I'm building a hotel procurement copilot—think ChatGPT, but for travel savings. LangChain agents, serverless architecture, embedding search—all designed to make enterprise decisions smarter and faster.

Let’s Build Something Meaningful

💬 I don’t chase hype—I build with empathy. If you’re working on something that matters, reach out.
