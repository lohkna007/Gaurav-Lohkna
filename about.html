Hi, I'm Gaurav

Ivy League labs taught me to ask questions, AI taught me to find answers—and I’m just getting started.

Curious Beginnings

In 2018, I embarked on a curious detour into neuroscience research at Yale. Little did I know, this exploration into the human brain would spark a much deeper question: how does intelligence truly work? And more importantly, could machines also learn and reason?

The Foundations

My foundational journey continued with a Computer Science degree in Delhi. Here, my focus extended beyond mere coding; I was driven to understand the underlying patterns of systems and the profound reasons why we engineer machines in the first place.

The Acceleration

The acceleration began when I joined the Berkeley x Plaksha AI/ML program. This intensive experience was my launchpad into applied machine learning, where I built innovative systems like a news recommender and led teams focused on harmonizing AI capabilities with human intuition.

Healthcare and Responsibility

At Minfy, I had the profound opportunity to apply AI in healthcare. I developed LLM-driven tools that significantly streamlined hospital operations, reducing patient discharge reporting time from 30 minutes to just 2 minutes. This experience reinforced that AI's true power lies in creating tangible benefits for real people.

The Copilot Era

My current focus at HRS Group is on pioneering the 'copilot era' for enterprise. I'm building an intelligent hotel procurement copilot—envision a specialized AI assistant for maximizing travel savings. This involves leveraging LangChain agents, designing robust serverless architectures, and implementing advanced embedding search to empower smarter, faster decision-making for businesses.

Let’s Build Something Meaningful

I believe in building with purpose, not just chasing trends. If you're passionate about creating solutions that truly matter, I'd love to connect and explore how we can build something impactful together.
