Hi, I'm Gaurav

Ivy League labs taught me to ask questions, AI taught me to find answers—and I’m just getting started.

Beyond the Timeline: My Story in AI

The Curiosity that Sparked a Journey

My adventure in technology began at the University of Delhi, where I dove deep into Computer Science. It was more than just learning code; it was about understanding how systems work, how they could be built, and the immense power they hold to shape our world. That foundational curiosity set me on a path I'm still passionately exploring today.

Asking the Right Questions, Finding AI's Answers

The real turning point was my Masters in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Plaksha University, in collaboration with UC Berkeley. This wasn't just about gaining knowledge; it was about cultivating a mindset. I learned to dissect complex problems, to ask the incisive questions, and then, to architect intelligent systems that could truly provide the answers. It was a rigorous journey, culminating in an Outstanding Capstone Award – a moment that truly affirmed I was on the right track.

Turning Insights into Impact: My First Steps in the Field

My early professional roles were all about getting my hands dirty and seeing AI's direct impact. At Minfy and during my initial stint at HRS Group as a Data Scientist, I focused on building practical, scalable solutions.

  • I crafted real-time dashboards that brought clarity to complex data, significantly cutting down manual reporting tasks. It was incredibly rewarding to see that 95% reduction in effort!
  • In healthcare, I developed AI-driven platforms that streamlined chronic disease management. It felt good to know my work was helping to decrease discharge reporting time from 30 minutes to under 2 minutes, and improve healthcare efficiency by 40%.

These experiences taught me the importance of not just building, but building with a purpose – directly solving real-world challenges.

Shaping Tomorrow: The Frontier of Generative AI

Now, as a Data & Machine Learning Engineer back at HRS Group, I'm deep into the exciting world of Generative AI and cloud-native solutions. My focus is on creating systems that aren't just smart, but truly transformative.

  • With the Hotel Procurement Copilot, I leveraged AWS Bedrock, LangChain, and RAG to build an intelligent assistant. The goal? To drastically cut down the time travel managers spend on procurement research. We saw an 80% reduction in analysis time and a 60% decrease in reliance on external consultants – it’s about empowering smarter decisions.
  • Then there's the Hotel Rebooking System, an AI-powered solution that constantly monitors hotel rates. It proactively rebooks lower-priced reservations, securing an average saving of €38 per reservation for travelers. It's a testament to how AI can quietly deliver significant value.

These projects, built with a strong emphasis on LLMOps and scalable AWS architectures, are where my passion for innovation truly comes alive.

My Core Belief: Building with Purpose

At the heart of everything I do is a simple belief: AI should simplify, empower, and enhance. My journey, from understanding core computer science to pioneering Generative AI solutions, has always been about translating complex ideas into tangible, impactful systems. I find immense satisfaction in optimizing operations, creating intuitive user experiences, and unearthing valuable insights from data. I'm grateful for every challenge that has shaped me, and I’m always looking for the next opportunity to build something truly meaningful.
