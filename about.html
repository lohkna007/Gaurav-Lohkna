Hi, I'm Gaurav

Ivy League labs taught me to ask questions, AI taught me to find answers—and I’m just getting started.

Beyond the Timeline: My Story in AI

The Spark: A Journey Begins

My exploration into the world of technology kicked off at the University of Delhi, diving deep into Computer Science. It wasn't just about learning algorithms and code; it was about nurturing a profound curiosity for how systems operate, how they're crafted, and their incredible potential to reshape our lives. That initial spark ignited a passion I'm still eagerly pursuing today.

Crafting Expertise: Asking the Right Questions, Finding AI's Answers

The true transformation happened during my Masters in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Plaksha University, in collaboration with UC Berkeley. This wasn't merely an academic pursuit; it was an intensive workshop in thinking differently. I learned to dissect complex problems, to ask the truly incisive questions, and then, crucially, to architect intelligent systems capable of delivering impactful answers. Earning the Outstanding Capstone Award was a humbling affirmation that I was indeed on the right path, pushing the boundaries of what AI could achieve.

From Code to Concrete: Driving Early Impact

My initial professional roles were all about getting my hands dirty and witnessing AI's tangible impact. As a Data Scientist at Minfy and during my first chapter at HRS Group, I focused on building scalable, practical solutions that made a real difference:

  • I engineered real-time dashboards that brought unparalleled clarity to complex data, remarkably slashing manual reporting tasks by 95%. Seeing that direct impact was incredibly motivating!
  • In the vital healthcare sector, I developed AI-driven platforms for chronic disease management. It's truly rewarding to know my work helped reduce discharge reporting time from 30 minutes to under 2 minutes, enhancing overall healthcare efficiency by 40%.

These experiences cemented my belief in building solutions that aren't just technically sound, but genuinely solve real-world pain points.

Innovating Today: The Frontier of Generative AI and Cloud-Native Solutions

Today, as a Data & Machine Learning Engineer back at HRS Group, I find myself at the forefront of designing and deploying intelligent systems that are truly redefining industry standards. My current focus revolves around cutting-edge Generative AI and robust cloud-native architectures:

  • I spearheaded the Hotel Procurement Copilot, leveraging AWS Bedrock, LangChain, and Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG). This intelligent assistant has already achieved an 80% reduction in procurement analysis time and a 60% decrease in reliance on costly external consultants. It's all about empowering smarter, faster business decisions.
  • I also engineered the Hotel Rebooking System, an AI-powered solution that continuously monitors hotel rates post-booking. It proactively identifies and secures lower prices, delivering an impressive average saving of €38 per reservation for travelers. This showcases how AI can deliver consistent, tangible value.

These projects, built with a deep emphasis on LLMOps and scalable AWS architectures, are where my passion for innovation truly comes alive, pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

My Core Belief: Building with Purpose and Passion

At the heart of every project I undertake is a simple, guiding principle: AI should simplify, empower, and profoundly enhance. My journey, from grasping foundational computer science concepts to pioneering advanced Generative AI solutions, has always been about translating intricate ideas into practical, impactful systems. I genuinely thrive on optimizing complex operations, crafting intuitive user experiences, and unearthing valuable, actionable insights from vast datasets. I'm incredibly grateful for every challenge that has shaped me, refining my skills and deepening my understanding. The world of AI is endlessly fascinating, and I'm always looking for the next opportunity to build something truly meaningful, contributing to a smarter, more efficient future.
